TUNE Summer Bridge to Research Program

The TUNE Summer Bridge to Research Program is a paid ten-week, full-time research experience for 20 first-time incoming community college transfer students to BCOE. Participants will receive a $5,000 stipend at the end of the ten weeks, given they have full-filled the time and research requirements. The program is supported by the Training from Undergraduate through Navy Engagement (TUNE) Grant from the Office of Naval Research, aimed at supporting underrepresented student populations in the engineering field. The summer bridge program will remove barriers to transfer by making students and faculty at the community college more familiar with life at UCR and more aware that a bachelors degree in engineering is within reach. The program requires a full-time commitment from each student for its entire duration. Participants are expected to work up to 40 hours/week in active research. Students will also be participating in a research symposium an oral presentation at the end of the ten-week program. The program dates are June 22-August 28, 2020.
